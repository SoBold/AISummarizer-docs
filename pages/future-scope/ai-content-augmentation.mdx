# Future Scope: AI Content Augmentation

## Overview

Automatically enhance content by generating additional context, examples, or related resources within the summaries.

## Key Features

-   **Context Expansion**: Add background information or examples relevant to the summarized content.
-   **Cross-Linking**: Suggest related posts or external resources dynamically.
-   **Audience-Specific Augmentation**: Tailor added content for specific audiences (e.g., beginners vs. experts).

## Benefits

-   Transforms summaries into comprehensive learning or resource hubs.
-   Enhances SEO by increasing internal linking and content depth.
