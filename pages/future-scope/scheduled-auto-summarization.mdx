# Future Scope: Scheduled Auto-Summarization

## Overview

Enable automatic summarization of content on a schedule, reducing manual intervention.

## Key Features

-   **Time-Based Triggers**: Schedule summaries to generate at specific times or intervals.
-   **Bulk Summarization**: Summarize all posts of selected types in a single batch process.
-   **Post-Publish Summarization**: Automatically summarize content immediately after publication.

## Benefits

-   Saves time for website admins managing frequent updates.
-   Ensures summaries are always up-to-date with the latest content.

<Callout type="info">
    `Note:` This will require cron jobs or similar scheduling mechanisms
</Callout>
